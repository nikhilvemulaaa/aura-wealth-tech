## 📌 Overview

**YONO AI** is a next-generation **AI-powered digital banking platform** designed to transform traditional banking into an intelligent financial ecosystem.

It combines **Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Automation, and Predictive Intelligence** to help users manage finances, track expenses, monitor investments, and receive personalized financial guidance in real time.

YONO AI enables users to move beyond basic banking and experience **proactive financial decision-making**.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional banking applications mainly focus on transactions such as:

- Checking account balance  
- Money transfers  
- Bill payments  
- Loan management  

However, users often struggle with:

❌ Lack of spending visibility  
❌ Poor financial planning  
❌ No personalized recommendations  
❌ Limited investment guidance  
❌ Low savings discipline  

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

YONO AI bridges the gap between **banking and financial intelligence**.

Using AI models and analytics, the system:

- Analyzes transaction behavior  
- Predicts future spending patterns  
- Detects unusual financial activity  
- Suggests better savings strategies  
- Recommends financial products  

This transforms banking into a **personal financial advisor**.
